The beauty of great photography is not just found in the pages of a magazine in faraway places, or among the most exotic landscapes. Rather it can often be in the subtle and mundane corners of life that remain unnoticed. It is the unnoticed such, as light captures the landscapes on an aging wall or emphasizes the detail on a random object that has been neglected or the brief moment where everything comes to rest in a busy environment. Such aspects, although right in front of us, inspire creativity and serve to highlight the fact that an extraordinary story can be conveyed through the commonplace.
